Sci-fi prompt tips

  • Start with the human — even in cosmic-scale stories, ground it in one person's perspective
  • Technology prompts work best when the tech has unintended consequences
  • First contact prompts: the alien should be genuinely alien, not a human in a suit
  • Ask: what does this technology or world do to relationships?
  • Hard SF writers: use the prompt as a premise, then research the science
